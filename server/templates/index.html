Mixwer - mixing answers

Blend multiple tests with this program:

  1. Upload one or more PDF files using the "Choose File" button.
  2. Click "Upload PDF" and wait for the process to complete (this may take a few minutes).
  3. Upon success, download your blended tests in a ZIP file.
